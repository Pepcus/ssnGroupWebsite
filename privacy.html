Pepcus Software Services Pvt. Ltd. Privacy Policy

Updated August 25, 2019

Pepcus Software Services Pvt. Ltd.is devoted to value your privacy. This Privacy Policy is intended to explain how personal information is used and processed at Pepcus’ website. This statement applies with respect to information we collect from http://pepcus.com/ and any other sites on the World Wide Web owned and/or operated by Pepcus Software. By visiting and using http://pepcus.com you accept the terms described below. Pepcus reserves the right to revise this statement and terms of use in the whole or in part at any time without any additional notifications, so please check this Policy at regular intervals.

Collecting Information

When you open this Website, Pepcus collects the IP address of the computer you are using to browse the site. If this address is a "static" or fixed IP address, Pepcus could in some conditions theoretically be able to identify you, and therefore, this type of information could be considered private data. However, Pepcus only gathers this kind of information to compile usage statistics on an anonymous basis; Pepcus does not collect IP address to recognize you.

Apart from your IP address, no private data is transferred when accessing this Website. Unless you submit personal data about yourself by filling in a form on the Website or by sending us an e-mail, we will not maintain any other personal data because of your access and use of the site.

Private Information Disclosure

Contact forms at http://pepcus.com/ require your name, e-mail, contact number and company name. We collect this information and use it for getting in touch with you only. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third parties. When involved in a joint marketing partnership, Pepcus may share information which you have submitted to us with the partner company which is subject to applicable non-disclosure obligations.

Use of "Cookies"

To assist you in a better way this Website sends "cookies" to your computer. A cookie is a small element of data that can be sent by our Website to your browser and stored on your hard drive, not on Pepcus’ Website. Cookies do not collect information about visitors. Unless you input your Personal Data onto our website you cannot be recognized and remain anonymous. You can set most browsers to tell you when you are sent a cookie and decline to receive it.

Security

Our site has advanced security measures to avoid data loss, mistreatment or modification. All your private information resides behind a firewall, and access to it is restricted to most of the Pepcus personnel (excluding managers that communicate with you directly).

Modifications to the Privacy Policy

Pepcus retains the right to variate, modify or otherwise update this Privacy Policy at any time. So, please check back at regular intervals.
